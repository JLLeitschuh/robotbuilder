Speed Controller

What is it?

A speed controller that uses PWM signals to control the speed of the attached motor. There are twelve main PWM speed controllers available for use in FRC: Victor, Talon, Jaguar, Victor SP, Talon SRX, Spark, SD540, Victor SPX, DMC 60, Spark Max, Talon FX, and the Venom Smart Motor.

Properties

Type
The type of speed controller to use.
Output Port (PWM)
The PWM port on the roboRIO or MXP that the output is plugged into. Ports 0-9 are on the roboRIO and 10-19 are on the MXP.
Inverted
Whether to invert the direction of the speed controller.
